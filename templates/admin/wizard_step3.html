The prompt tells the extension which field to fill on the login page. Use the noncey Chrome extension to pick the OTP input field on the target website.

1
Open the website where you enter the OTP code (the login page for {{ config.name }}).
2
Click the noncey icon in your browser toolbar, and then the small gear icon at the right-top corner to open the settings page of the browser extension.
3
Find {{ config.name }} in the configuration list and click Pick field.
4
Click the OTP input field on the page. The extension captures the selector and sends it here automatically.
